Submit

Have you watched or read anything recently that you want to review? Do you have a topic of interest that you want to write about? Well, you're in luck! All club members are welcome to submit posts, as long as they adhere to the following guidelines:

  • All content must be related to the anime subculture or Japanese culture in general. This includes (but is not limited to) anime, manga, American cartoons, video games, music, and fashion. If you are unsure if your post falls into this category, feel free to contact one of the officers.
  • Please keep all content appropriate. Submissions that are deemed otherwise will be rejected.
  • If your post contains spoilers, please label and put a visible warning.

    • All submissions will be reviewed and proofread by the CAB officers before being posted.
